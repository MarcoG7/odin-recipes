Lamb Souvlaki

Description

Juicy lamb souvlaki made with marinated pieces of lamb grilled to perfection. Skewers served with tzatziki sauce and pita bread, or garlic roasted potatoes and Greek salad.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup olive oil
  • 1½ tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1½ tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1½ tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1½ pounds boneless leg of lamb, trimmed of all fat and cut into 1-inch cubes
